What is Conoscenza 2018 ?

Conoscenza, The National Level Technical Symposium of St.Joseph's Institute of Technology, tests your inert abilities to Adhere, Persevere, Coolness, Bravery, Awesomeness and your abilities to follow strict rules and code of conduct. Are you strong enough to handle the heat? Be prepared to face your worst nightmares. May the force be with you!

General Instructions

1. All participants should bring their registration slip along with college id-card.
2. Participants will be permitted to enter the campus only in formal dress code.
3. College busses will be available from all parts of the city.
4. Registration will be closed by 8:30 AM.
5. Events will start at 9AM.
6. Breakfast and lunch will be provided by our college.
7. Mobile phones are strictly prohibited.
8. The symposium is open to all the departments of Engineering and Technology.
9. Proper decorum must be maintained in accordance with the college rules while inside the college campus.
